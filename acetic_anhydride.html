Hazard Classification

Corrosive & toxic by inhalation; flammable liquid; strong lachrymator

Acetic anhydride can cause severe skin burns and serious eye damage; inhalation of vapors/mists can be very dangerous. It reacts with moisture and releases heat and acetic acid. Use closed handling/ventilation, chemical-resistant gloves, goggles/face shield, and strict spill control. In many countries it is also treated as a controlled precursor for security/regulatory reasons—extra documentation and tracking may be required (often relevant for cross-border trade, including into/out of China).

Chronic Health Effects

Repeated exposure can worsen respiratory/skin sensitization; focus on engineering controls

Chronic or repeated exposure to irritating/corrosive vapors can aggravate respiratory conditions and cause persistent dermatitis. For large plants (cellulose acetate and acetylation hubs), best practice is local exhaust ventilation, closed transfer, corrosion-resistant equipment, and strict worker training—especially in fast-growing Asia supply chains where new capacity is added (including China).

Production Process & Capacity

Acetic anhydride is commonly produced via the ketene process (acetic acid → ketene → acetic anhydride) . integrated acetic acid chains and large downstream hubs (cellulose acetate, pharma).
1Feedstock Preparation
Glacial acetic acid is dried and purified. Utilities and corrosion-resistant materials are selected due to strong reactivity and moisture sensitivity.
2Ketene Generation
Acetic acid is thermally converted to ketene under controlled conditions. Heat integration and safety systems manage high temperatures and reactive intermediates.
3Acetylation / Formation
Ketene reacts with acetic acid to form acetic anhydride. Reaction control targets product quality while minimizing side reactions and hydrolysis.
4Distillation, Storage & Controls
Distillation removes residual acetic acid and impurities. Storage requires dry systems, sealed transfer, and compliance documentation in regulated trade (often emphasized for China-linked logistics).
